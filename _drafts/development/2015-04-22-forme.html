FormMe allows the creation and management of forms, in a simple way. You just need to setup your account, create a new form and point the action for it.

The objective of the product is mainly for learning and showcase purposes, and not to replace any of the good solutions out there (https://formkeep.com/, http://pooleapp.com/). In fact, I am using PooleApp, and it is more than enough for my needs (a contact form on my jekyll site), and this will have an impact on how I will deal with the development of the formMe.

I have 3 main features:

  1. Create an app to integrate with pooleApp and have a better visualization of the data

    PooleApp provides an api to create and fetch de data, using two different api keys. So for this I will need a way to setup my PooleApp visualization key. Also I want a better visualization then just the json.

  2. Allow the creation of forms independently of third party services
  3. Migrate the front-end of the app to use AngularJS, or other similar technology
